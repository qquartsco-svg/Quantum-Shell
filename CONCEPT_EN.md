# Quantum Shell Foundation — Concept

> **English.** Korean (정본): [CONCEPT.md](CONCEPT.md)

## Why layer B

The Electromagnetic Boundary Foundation (A) describes **classical charge and boundaries**.  
Electrostatics alone does **not** yield electron shells or discrete energy levels — that step requires quantum mechanics, as stated in the A-layer concept doc.

This package occupies **layer B** immediately above A.

## Physical scope

- **Hydrogenic approximation:** effective nuclear charge and principal quantum number n.
- **Orbital basis:** subshell degeneracy including spin (textbook form).
- **Screening:** Slater-**like** proxy, not a full Slater table.
- **Aufbau:** Z ≤ 118, non-relativistic filling order.
- **Ionization proxy:** hydrogenic binding vs thermal energy and de Broglie length — **order-of-magnitude** use only.

## Toward layer C

Covalent/ionic bonding, molecular orbitals, and solvent effects belong in a **chemistry / interface** foundation.  
This repo stops at **atomic quantum shells**.

## One sentence

> **The Coulomb potential is layer A; the shell structure electrons occupy is screened in layer B.**
